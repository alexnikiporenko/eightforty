840: New Music for String Quartet

7.30pm, 6 June 2015 at The Pound, Corsham

840: New Music for String Quartet travelled to The Pound in Corsham, Wilstshire on the 6 June 2015. The concert featured The Manon Quartet presenting new pieces by Louis d'Heudieres, Matthew Lee Knowles, Neil Luck, Alex Nikiporenko, Nicholas Peters and Tristan Rhys Williams, as well as Doubles (2012) by John Lely.

The Manon Quartet are: Jian Ren, Fiona Robertson, Morag Robertson and Deni Teo.
